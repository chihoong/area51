# Breakfast Oats

## Servings
- makes 1 serving

## Ingredients

- 150g cooked steal cut oatmeal (cooked in water)
- 75g 0% strauss milk
- 25g dates
- 80g berries (mix of blueberries, strawberries and blackberries)
- 8g hemp seeds
- 8g ground black sesame
- 25g nut butter (mix of almonds and hazelnuts, no sugar added)
