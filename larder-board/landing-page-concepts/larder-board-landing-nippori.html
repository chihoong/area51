The problem

Every operator feels a slow week. Almost none know whether that's their problem or the whole market's. A competitor opened. A local event fell through. You'd make entirely different decisions if you knew.

The approach

Anonymous aggregation of Square POS data across restaurants in your local market. You contribute, you receive — trust built in from day one.
