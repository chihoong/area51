The problem

Context you're flying without

Every operator feels a slow week — but almost none of them know whether that's on them or on the market. A competitor opened. A local event fell through. Tourism dipped. You'd make entirely different decisions if you knew.

Larder Board anonymously pools Square POS data across restaurants — giving you the benchmarking context you currently have no reliable way to get. See how your sales, order volume, and ticket size compare against similar operators in your market, updated in real time.

How it works

Three steps. Zero dashboards.

You run the restaurant. We surface the context you've always been missing.

1
Connect

Link your Square account. No data entry, no exports, no spreadsheet gymnastics. We read your POS data automatically and anonymize it before it ever enters the pool.

2
Compare

See your sales, order volume, and average ticket benchmarked against similar restaurants in your market. Filtered by cuisine type, location, and size — not vague national averages.

3
Decide

Know whether your slow Tuesday is a you problem or a market problem — then act accordingly. Reprice. Extend hours. Stay the course. Make decisions with actual context behind them.
