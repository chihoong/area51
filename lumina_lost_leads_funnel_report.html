Action plan

1

Fix subject line — this is the #1 lever

"Lumina's pricing just got easier" is self-referential and low urgency. Lost leads don't remember Lumina and don't care that pricing changed. The subject needs to answer why they should open right now. Test: $29/month per kiosk — no setup fees or Still thinking about kiosk ordering? or We made it cheaper. Here's what changed.

Priority 1
2

Clean the list before the next send

6.8% bounce rate is 3× the acceptable threshold. Left unaddressed this will damage sender reputation and compound open rate problems. Run the lost leads list through ZeroBounce or NeverBounce and suppress all invalid addresses before sending again. Also check: cold leads list has 10.6% bounce — needs the same treatment.

Priority 1
3

Audit the landing page CTA

21 UTM visitors, 2 clicked, avg 23s on page. The post-click experience is not working. Check onlumina.com/pricing — is there a single, obvious CTA above the fold? The flow should be: see price → one button → talk to someone or start. Remove any decision paralysis (multiple plans with no recommended default, no social proof, no urgency).

Priority 1
4

Strengthen the email body copy

The current email is 4 sentences with no social proof, no concrete value statement, and no reason to believe $29/month is competitive. Add: what $29/month gets them specifically, a one-line social proof (e.g. "Used by Ichiran across their NYC locations"), and a single clear CTA button instead of a plain text link.

Priority 2
5

Separate lost leads and cold leads messaging

Both segments are receiving near-identical pricing broadcasts. Lost leads need a re-engagement angle — acknowledge the gap, lead with what changed. Cold leads need a credibility-first intro — they don't know Lumina at all. These are fundamentally different audiences and should have different sequences, not the same one-shot blast.

Priority 2
6

Replace one-shot broadcasts with a 3-email nurture sequence

One-touch cold re-engagement rarely converts. Build a 3-email automated campaign for lost leads: Email 1 — re-introduction + what changed (pricing). Email 2 (3 days later) — social proof + customer story. Email 3 (7 days later) — direct ask with a time-bound offer. Measure conversion at each step and kill what doesn't work.
