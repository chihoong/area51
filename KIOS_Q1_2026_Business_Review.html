Key Observations
01 — Volume

Significant order & location decline

Orders fell 28% YoY and active locations dropped 36% (242 → ~155). A third of KIOS locations churned or went inactive between Q1 '25 and Q1 '26.

02 — AOV

Average order value up 17%

AOV rose from $19.88 to $23.24 — the standout positive metric. Shift toward higher-ticket concepts like Ichiran is likely the driver.

03 — Concentration

40% of revenue from 3 locations

Ichiran's 3 NYC locations account for ~$5.3M of $13.2M net sales. Extreme concentration risk for the channel.

04 — Trend

March recovery is real

March was the strongest month at $4.87M net, 213K orders. February dip appears seasonal — no structural deterioration within Q1.

05 — Pricing

Ichiran subscription underpriced

Ichiran pays $39K/quarter flat on $4.87M gross. At standard 1.5% they'd owe ~$73K — a $34K/quarter discount relative to volume.

06 — Take Rate

2.0% blended Lumina take rate

$237K on $11.96M gross. Above standard 1.5% due to subscription revenue on lower-volume accounts. Room to improve through repricing.
